Welcome to Zone031!

Zone031 is a laser tag club based in Gothenburg, Sweden. We actively work to promote laser tag as a sport and are always keen to recruit new members. Currently we have around 20 active members with more occassionals. We train regularly three times a week and our Thursday sessions are always open to those who find themselves interested in seeing what playing laser tag in a more structured manner is about. Did we mention that your first training session is entirely free of charge? So there is no reason to hesitate! Fill out the form below and you will receive invites to our regular thursday session. If you want to contact us with any questions beforehand, the easiest way is to send a message to our facebook page linked below and someone will reply promptly! If you simply want to see what we're about check out our twitch streams from our training sessions and our instagram account.

Inside the Hisingen arena

Sign up for open training invitations every Thursday!

Every thursday we host beginner-friendly training sessions at Laserdome Hisingen. First time is free! This is a great opportunity to come and find out what competetive lasertag is about and get introduced to lasertag as a sport. As this is a mixed training group you will encounter all levels of opposition while at the same time getting tips from some of the best lasertag players in Europe.
