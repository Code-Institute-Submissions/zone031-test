Welcome to Zone031!

Zone031 is a lasertag association based in Gothenburg, Sweden. We actively work to promote lasertag as a sport and are always keen to recruit new members.

Sign up for open training invitations every Thursday!

Every thursday we host beginner-friendly training sessions at Laserdome Hisingen. This is a great opportunity to come and find out what competetive lasertag is about and get introduced to lasertag as a sport. As this is a mixed training group you will encounter all levels of opposition while at the same time getting tips from some of the best lasertag players in Europe.
