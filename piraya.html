Feydakin

The mythos of the Feydakin players is as dark as the arena during power failure. The earthly counterpart to the death commandos Fedaykin of Arrakis, Feydakin excel at splitting defences, destroying bases and eliminating their opponents.

Feydakin boasts almost a century of collective player experience. As well as being some of the best players in the world, every Feydakin player is also an innovator having honed and developed a unique playstyle from scratch.
