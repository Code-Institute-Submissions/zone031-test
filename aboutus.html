About us

Zone031 is a laser tag club founded in 2009. We operate independently on the premises of Laserdome Hisingen. We host regular training activities on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Sundays starting from 18:45. We have decades and decades of lasertag experience put together and are dedicated to introducing the sport to new players. If you are interested in trying out competetive laser tag, do not hesitate to contact us. The easiest way to do that is to send us a message on facebook!
