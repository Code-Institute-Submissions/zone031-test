Tournaments hosted by Zone031

Masters Meeting

Masters Meeting is an annual team competition quad tournament. The tournament has been around for two decades and is together with The Gathering in Stockholm one of the two main open tournaments in Sweden. Outside of nationals Masters Meeting is without a doubt one of the most prestigous tournaments around.

Masters Meeting 2020

Masters Meeting 2020 was the first Masters Meeting hosted at Laserdome Hisingen. The event saw 8 teams competing in the two main events of Team Competition and Elimination and even more in the baserun event. Players from Sweden, Finland, Germany and France attended the event.

Double Trouble

Double trouble was a small tournament played in the summer of 2020. The tournament was played on four different doubles formats. Smiley & Slaanesh were the winners of the tournament, Gottemaja & Mumin placed second and Bambi & Paulina third.
